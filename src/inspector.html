How to use

Ticker field supports Finvinz website: e.g.

  Stocks

    •AAPL - Apple
    •GOOGL - Alphabet

  Forex

    •EURUSD - EUR/USD
     •XAUUSD - XAU/USD

  Commodities

    •GC - Gold
    •SI - Silver

  Futures

    •ES - E-mini S&P 500
    •NQ - E-mini Nasdaq 100

  Crypto

    Ticker field supports CoinMarketCap website.
    You have to get the name from the url: e.g.

    •BITCOIN - coinmarketcap.com/currencies/bitcoin
    •ETHEREUM - coinmarketcap.com/currencies/ethereum
    •THE-GRAPH - coinmarketcap.com/currencies/the-graph

Show as: Add custom ticker - e.g GOLD

Frequency: How often the stock prices are fetched

  •On push - Every time the key button is pressed.
  •5 minutes - Every 5 minutes.
  •30 minutes - Every 30 minutes.
  •1 hour - Hourly.
